About us

Our Story

For fifteen wonderful years, Mama Rosa's has been serving the heart and soul of Italian cuisine to our beloved community. What started as a dream in Mama Rosa's kitchen has blossomed into a cherished gathering place where families create memories over authentic Italian meals.

Our restaurant was founded on the simple belief that food brings people together. Every dish we serve is crafted from recipes passed down through three generations of our family, using the same love and care that Mama Rosa put into her cooking. From our hand-rolled pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, everything is made fresh daily in our kitchen.

What Makes Us Special

At Mama Rosa's, you're not just a customer ‚Äì you're family. Our warm, inviting atmosphere is perfect for romantic dinners, family celebrations, or casual lunches with friends. We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients, many sourced locally, to create dishes that honor traditional Italian cooking while delighting modern palates.

Whether you're craving a classic spaghetti carbonara, a perfectly crispy margherita pizza, or our famous homemade tiramisu, every bite tells the story of our heritage. Our friendly staff treats every guest like family, ensuring your dining experience is memorable from the moment you walk through our doors.

Join Our Family

We invite you to experience the warmth of Italian hospitality at Mama Rosa's. Whether you're dining in our cozy restaurant, enjoying takeout at home, or letting us cater your special event, we're committed to serving you meals made with passion, authenticity, and love.
